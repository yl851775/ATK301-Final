OBOIST

TZU-HAN HU

Tzu-Han is a Taiwanese oboist. She was born in 1991 in Taitung, Taiwan.
In 2016, she graduated from National Chiao Tung University (NCTU) in Taiwan. Now, Tzu-Han studys in Music Therapy at Illinois State University (ISU).
During the years in Taiwan, Tzu-Han received first prize from Taipei City Music Competition and had experiences performing with several orchestras. She was the member in National Symphony Orchestra (NSO) Academy as well as TMUE Orchestra. In addition, she was a certified secondary school teacher in Taiwan and served as an oboe section instructor in many high schools.
